Attention Canadians: Continuum Approved by Royal College!

April 2017

Volume 23 / Issue 2

As of the April 2017 issue, Continuum is an Accredited Self-Assessment Program (Section 3) as defined by the Maintenance of Certification Program of the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada, and approved by the Office of Continuing Medical Education and Professional Development, University of Calgary. Canadian participants can claim a maximum of 14 hours (credits are automatically calculated). Go to our CME page for more information.
